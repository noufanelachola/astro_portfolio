I'm Noufan Elachola, a final-year Computer Science and Engineering student from Kerala, India.

Over time, my interests have gradually shifted towards Operational Technology (OT) and Industrial Control Systems (ICS) security. What interested me about this field is that it brings together computer science, electrical and electronics engineering.

At the moment, I'm learning through hands-on labs, technical documentation, research papers, and personal projects. I enjoy understanding how industrial systems work, how they communicate, and how they can be protected from cyber threats.
