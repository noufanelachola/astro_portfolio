I'm Noufan Elachola, a final year Computer Science and Engineering student 
with a strong interest in Operational Technology (OT) and Industrial Control Systems (ICS) Security.

My interest in engineering started long before university. 
My father and brothers have always been interested in electronics and electrical work, and they were the ones who first introduced me to electronics, Arduino, and 
basic electrical systems. 

Growing up, I often assisted them with household electrical work, which gave me early hands on exposure and sparked my curiosity in electrical and electronics.

I completed my secondary and senior secondary education at Ma'din Public School before pursuing my undergraduate degree in Computer Science and Engineering at 
MEA Engineering College. 

During my studies, I developed an interest in Machine Learning and enjoyed building projects that combined both Machine Learning and Electronics. 
Later I got the oppurtunity to work as an intern in a research project at National Institute of Technology (NIT), Calicut, Kerala.  

As I explored different areas of technology, I came across Operational Technology (OT) and Industrial Control Systems (ICS), a field that immediately caught my attention.

Since then, I have been learning and building my skills in this area, and I later had the opportunity to work as an OT Security Intern at 
Veermata Jijabai Technological Institute (VJTI), Mumbai, Maharashtra.
